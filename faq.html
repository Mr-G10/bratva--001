What is Forex?

The word Forex is derived from the words Foreign Exchange. Basically it refers to the exchange of currencies.

What is the Forex Market?

The Forex market is the largest financial market in the world where currencies are traded and exchanged. It has an approximate daily trading volume of $5.3 Trillion Dollars.
